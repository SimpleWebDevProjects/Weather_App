About WeatherPulse

About This App

WeatherPulse is a modern weather application that provides real-time weather updates for cities worldwide. With a clean, intuitive interface and a professional design aesthetic, it offers both functionality and visual appeal.

Key features include city search, detailed weather information, 5-day forecasts, temperature charts, and the ability to pin your favorite cities for quick access to their current conditions.

Weather Facts

The highest temperature ever recorded on Earth was 56.7°C in Death Valley, California in 1913.

Snowflakes falling at 2-4 mph can take about 1 hour to reach the ground from cloud level.
